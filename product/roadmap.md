# Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation
- Refine the vision
- Define the culture (what does "good fan" mean? what's our vibe?)
- Define the scope of MVP (both tech and community features)

## Phase 2: Technical Decisions
- Pick basic architecture choices
- Choose tech stack for vibecoding

## Phase 3: Vibecode Fast
- Build MVP quickly
- Focus on working > perfect

## Phase 4: Community Building
- Get people using it
- Make friends
- Learn what actually works

## Phase 5: Rebuild
- Rebuild properly, piece by piece
- Do it under the functioning community
- Handcrafted engineering where it matters
